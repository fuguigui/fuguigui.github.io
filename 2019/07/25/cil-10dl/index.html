Speech denoise

The record is composed of the original speech and interferer. We want to remove the interferer and leave the original speech.

Method

  1. learn dictionaries for speech and interferer specifically.
  2. Encoding the measured signal on these two dictionaries .
  3. Drop the encoded signals on the interferer dictionary, only keep the speech encoded signals .
  4. Recover the “pure” signal
